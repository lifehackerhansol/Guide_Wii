    This guide covers dumping GameCube or Wii game discs to a local SD card or USB drive on your Wii.

    Dump di un Disco su SD/USB (CleanRip)

    Requisiti

    • Una scheda SD o unità USB con almeno 4.7 GB di spazio disponibile (8.5 GB per il dump di un disco a doppio strato).
    • CleanRip

    Preparazione

    1. Spegni la console.
    2. Inserisci la scheda SD o dispositivo USB nel tuo computer.
    3. Copia la cartella apps dal .zip di CleanRip al root della tua scheda SD o unità USB.
    4. Reinserisci la tua scheda SD o dispositivo USB nella tua console.
    5. Accendi la console.
    6. Avvia l’Homebrew Channel.
    7. Avvia CleanRip dalla lista degli homebrew.

    8. When prompted, select Yes to calculate disc checksums.

    9. Seleziona il dispositivo su cui eseguirai il dump del gioco - unità USB o scheda SD.

    10. Select the filesystem type of the device - in most cases it will be FAT (FAT32).

    11. Ensure that your device is connected to the system and press A to proceed.

    12. Quando richiesto, seleziona Yes per scaricare i file DAT di redump.org.
      • Questo è richiesto per assicurarsi che i dump siano puliti/accurati.

      Potresti ottenere un errore se lo fai. If so, restart your Wii and try again - but if you continue to get exception errors, dump the disc and verify the checksum in Dolphin instead if needed.

    13. Insert the game disc you would like to dump, and press A to continue.

    Dumping a Wii Disc

    As of v2.1.2 of CleanRip, there is an option to auto-detect whether or not the disc you have is dual layer. If you are on an earlier version, or just want to verify that the correct option is being selected, you can check this list to see if your game is a dual layer disc.

    If you get an unrecovered read error, ensure that the disc you are attempting to dump is clean and can be properly read by the console. Even though you may be able to play the game disc normally, you may still get this error. If the error persists, try to dump another disc and see if the same error happens.

    1. When prompted, select if your disc is an unlicensed Datel disc. If unsure, select No.

    2. Regola le impostazioni come mostrato sotto, nel frattempo verifica se il disco è a doppio strato o no.

    3. Premi A per iniziare il dump del disco.
      • This process can take quite some time, since it will dump all 4.7 GB of the DVD (8.5 GB for dual layer discs).

    4. When the dump finishes, you should see a screen with the MD5 checksum of your disc, compared to the Redump DAT if you enabled it.

    Proceed to Managing Wii Backups to join the PART files that are output, and manage your dumped games.

    Dumping a GameCube Disc

    1. When prompted, select if your disc is an unlicensed Datel disc. If unsure, select No.

    2. Premi A per iniziare il dump del disco.
      • This process can take some time, since it will dump all 1.3 GB of the Mini DVD.

    3. When the dump finishes, you should see a screen with the MD5 checksum of your disc, compared to the Redump DAT if you enabled it.

    Proceed to Managing GameCube Backups to shrink the size of your output file, and manage your dumped games.
